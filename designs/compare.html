BAM Control - Design Comparison

Two design directions for the BAM Control webapp redesign. Each variation includes a Dashboard view and a Site Detail view. Click the tabs to compare.

Variation A - Key Traits

  • Deep navy background (#0f172a)
  • Electric blue accent (#3b82f6)
  • Emerald green for success states
  • Premium SaaS feel (Linear, Vercel, Railway)
  • Glassmorphism effects on panels
  • Alternating table row backgrounds
  • High contrast stat cards
  • Subtle shadows for depth

Variation B - Key Traits

  • Warm white background (#fafaf9)
  • Warm indigo accent (#6366f1)
  • Teal for success, amber for warnings
  • Calm professional feel (Notion, Stripe)
  • Minimal shadows, border-driven hierarchy
  • Clean tables with thin dividers
  • Generous whitespace and breathing room
  • Soft colored status badges
